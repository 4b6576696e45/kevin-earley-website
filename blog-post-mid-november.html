Blog Post: Mid November

Wow. How is it that we are already 1 week out from thanksgiving? I’ve certainly been busy in the past few weeks. Josh and I have been going out on our usual schooldudes, replacing printers, toner, keyboards etc. But we also have taken on a rather unique project from Ms. Collins, to create an introductory video for the Massachusetts School Library Association Bookmark Contest. This has really exercised my video editing skills, using DoInk, for green screens, and iMovie and Premiere for editing. This has been a very neat alternative to normal schooldudes and it would be interesting to do more videos in the future. Our genius hour projects are also progressing. I am trying to further open discussion inside the town historical society, for updating it’s web presence and it seems that things are progressing nicely. That’s all for now. Happy Thanksgiving everyone!
